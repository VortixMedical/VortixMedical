Who We Are

Vortix Medical excels in delivering high-quality medical hardware and comprehensive solutions.

We oversee the entire production process, from vendor selection to logistics and warehousing, ensuring that products are stored and transported safely, in compliance with all laws and regulations. Our service extends to managing the financial aspects of these operations, streamlining the transition from production to retail. Vortix Medical’s all-inclusive approach allows retailers to enhance their product range with white-label offerings and simplifies complex supply chains.

What we do

Dedicated to empowering the healthcare retail sector, we enable our clients to focus on what they do best—providing exceptional products and services to their customers.

Product Development

Vortix Medical excels in crafting top-tier medical hardware and tailored solutions for retailers seeking white-label products. From meticulous vendor selection to rigorous testing, we ensure each product meets the highest quality and regulatory standards.

Logistics Management

Efficient logistics are at the heart of Vortix Medical's operations. Our dedicated team orchestrates seamless warehousing, transportation, and distribution, guaranteeing safe and timely product delivery while upholding strict regulatory compliance

Compliance

The Department of Regulatory Compliance for Health ensures compliance with all regulations and standards related to the development, manufacturing, importation, storage, and distribution of health products.

Financial Integration

Leveraging financial data and systems across the company’s operations to optimize profitability, manage risks, and comply with regulatory requirements in the medical products industry.
